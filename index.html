Projects

Selected work

Modal title

A division at AbbVie had hired leadership training consultants, who shared good ideas, but there was no traction for improvement. They wanted something different and something that would actually result in positive change. NextMove promised something different. What makes us unique is that rather than training about high performance, NextMove engages leaders and team members in practicing high performance. 45 leaders engaged in our unique movement activities, which allowed them to try on a new leadership mindset. They embodied a learner’s mindset, developed a sense of ‘we’, and experienced the vulnerability required to lead top performing teams. Following this, leaders identified key changes they felt would create the positive change they wanted. Using our Our Change Up tool, groups clarified the outcomes they were aiming for and then designed mini- experiments they could implement the next day. In addition, the developed a plan to review their experiments, learn, and evolve their way to excellence—with skillful practice over time.
